(Draft) Agenda

The WoT-Workshop and a previous opening day will take place from June 3rd to June 5th in Munich. Please note that this page is constantly updated.

3 June 2019: Open Day

Opening day with the topic 'Open Standardards for Smart Systems'. The opening day will include

  • keynote speakers,
  • plenum discussion,
  • live demonstrators, and
  • social event.
