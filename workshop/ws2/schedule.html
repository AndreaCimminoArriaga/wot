WoT Workshop Schedule

The WoT workshop and a preceding opening day is taking place in Munich from 3. June until 5. June 2019.

Detail draft agenda will be updated asap

3. June 2019: Open Day

Opening day with the topic ‘Open Standardization as Key Enabler for Smart Systems'. The opening day will have

  • keynote speakers,
  • live demonstrators,
  • plenum discussion,
  • social event.

4. June 2019: WoT Workshop Day I

Presentation of papers (CfP).

5. June 2019: WoT Workshop Day II

Summary and identification of key messages from presentations of the first day. This day should also cover

  • breakout sessions
  • chartering discussion for WoT WG
